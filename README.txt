# InspiNova

**InspiNova** is an innovative company specialized in the creation and installation of advanced universal driving assistance devices. Our solutions integrate cutting-edge technologies such as sensors, infrared cameras, and microcontrollers powered by artificial intelligence to enhance driving safety and experience.

## 🌟 Mission

Our mission is to **make driving accessible, secure, and comfortable for everyone**, including people with disabilities. We offer technologies tailored to specific needs to ensure an inclusive driving experience.

## 🚀 Services

We provide a complete range of services designed to improve safety and driving experience:

1. **Installation of driving assistance devices**  
   Professional and fast service to integrate our advanced devices into any vehicle.

2. **Continuous map and software updates**  
   Subscribe to our premium service for automatic updates that improve detection systems and compatibility.

3. **24/7 technical assistance and support**  
   Our expert team is available to solve problems and offer personalized training.

4. **Collaborations with car manufacturers**  
   We work with manufacturers to integrate our technologies into new vehicles.

5. **Device customization**  
   We tailor our systems to meet specific needs, such as visual alerts, detection adjustments, and custom interface options.

## 💡 Added Value

- **For people with disabilities**:  
  - Misunderstandings: complementary visual and tactile signals.  
  - Low visibility conditions: enhanced detection systems.  
  - Specific adaptations for safer driving.

- **Economic and environmental impact**:  
  - Reuse of existing vehicles, reducing costs.  
  - Fewer accidents thanks to better risk prevention.

## 🌍 Long-term Vision

We aim to make **InspiNova** an essential player in the field of inclusive mobility technologies, contributing to a more equitable and accessible society.
